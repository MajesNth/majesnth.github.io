Welcome

My motivation

In high school, I did better at subjects like Math and sciences because they were more logical and needed less memorization than the other subjects, and my teachers often recommended that I go for an engineering program after high school. Software engineering seemed like the most interesting to me, partly because I spend a lot of time on computers playing video games and I thought that maybe I could learn how to make them, or even if I don't pursue gaming development learn how to make software because it is now a big part of our daily lives, therefore having lots of applications and job opportunities. There are also globally more positions for Software Engineers than Game Developpers, especially in my home country.

Why the ICT programme suits me

I already started a Software Engineering program previously, so I am already familiar with a lot of concepts. I liked it and generally performed well, therefore even though I did not previously succeed, it is what I wanted to continue. I failed mainly due to a lack of professional attitude and having that experience motivates me not to make the same mistakes and take things seriously this time around.
