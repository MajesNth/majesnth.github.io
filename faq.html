Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
  1. Open the following link in your browser: https://hz.mynetpay.nl/
  2. Log in with your HZ username and password
  3. Tap Web Print
  4. At Step 1: Select a printer, check the settings (format, single/double sided etc.)
  5. Click in the field at Step 2: Select files to choose your files (you can also drag and drop)
  6. Click on Upload files now Your file has now been sent to the appropriate printer. Use your HZ card at the printer to print the document.
  1. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. Log on to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ user name & password.
  2. Choose the Scan option in the menu.
  3. Place the original(s) on the feeder or glass plate.
  4. Press Start
  5. After scanning, press the following symbol on the printer to log off
  6. Check your HZ mailbox for the scanned document
  1. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
  1. Go to webshop.hz.nl
  2. Change the language to English if needed
  3. Sign in if needed
  4. Find the item you want to buy
  5. Add it to your cart
  6. Select your payment method (Paypal or iDEAL)
  7. Confirm your payment
  1. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
  1. Open the help desk reachable from the portal.hz.nl services section.
  2. Select "Make a reservation" option.
  3. Select "Reserve meeting room or Project group room".
  4. Click on "Reserve a room".
  5. Select the room and time slot
  6. Enter required details such as a brief description and number of participants
  7. Confirm the reservation.
  1. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
  • On the site at the Poelendaelesingel 10 (formerly the PZEM site) 100 parking spaces are available for students, staff and visitors.
  • The parking lot is only a 300 meter walk to the location Het Groene Woud. You can use the pedestrian tunnel.
  • You get access to the parking lot with your HZ-pass. After passing the barrier, follow the signs to the HZ parking lots. The HZ parking spaces have a white sign with the HZ logo. There are also a number of spaces available for recharging an electric car
  • Employees can register visitors through the Selfservice portal as before. Please mention 'please park on HZ terrain' in the comments field. Upon arrival, visitors report via the intercom.
  • Disabled parking Right in front of the HZ building there are two disabled parking lots. Use of these can be coordinated with the reception in Middelburg.
  • If you have any questions, contact Martine van Ommen, facilities team leader.
